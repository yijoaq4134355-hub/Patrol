关于2025年正月十四巡境活动路线安排的通知 Notice on the Route Arrangement for the "Xunjing" Activity on the 14th Day of the First Lunar Month, 2025

各村民小组、相关农户：To all village groups and relevant households:

为切实保障本村传统民俗活动"巡境"安全有序开展，经村委会研究并报镇文化站备案，现将2025年正月十四巡境活动路线及相关事项通知如下： To ensure the safe and orderly execution of our village's traditional folk activity "Xunjing", after deliberation by the Village Committee and filing with the Town Culture Station, the route arrangement and relevant matters for the 2025 activity on the 14th day of the first lunar month are hereby notified as follows:

一、活动基本信息I. Basic Activity Information

  • 活动名称：鹤田村2025年正月十四传统巡境仪式Activity Name: Hetian Village 2025 Traditional Xunjing Ceremony
  • 活动时间：农历正月十四（公历2025年2月11日），上午8:00至下午17:00Time: 14th day of the first lunar month (February 11, 2025), 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • 安全责任人：周建国（村委文书） [代签]Safety Officer: Zhou Jianguo [Signed by Proxy]

三、路线调整说明III. Route Adjustment Explanation

因北坡区域近期正在进行道路及地下管网拓宽施工，原定于北坡地块深处的神坛停驻点无法正常使用。为防范地质沉降风险，经与施工方协商，临时增设外围的"北坡临时祭点"作为替代方案。各队伍行进至此时须严格遵循标记路线，切勿深入施工盲区。 Due to road and underground pipe widening construction in the North Slope area, the original altar deep within the site cannot be used. To prevent geological settlement risks, a "North Slope Temporary Altar" on the periphery is added as an alternative. All teams must strictly follow the marked route and must not enter blind construction zones.

四、活动安全与管理要求IV. Safety and Management Requirements

  1. 消防安全：活动期间严禁在非指定区域燃放烟花爆竹，祭祀用火需由专人看管，确保人走火灭。Fire Safety: Fireworks are strictly prohibited outside designated areas. Ritual fires must be supervised.
  2. 秩序维护：请各村民小组长协助维持现场秩序，避免人员拥挤、踩踏。未经允许，外来人员不得随意进入祭祀核心区。Order Maintenance: Group leaders please assist in maintaining order. Unauthorized outsiders are not allowed in the core ritual area.
  3. 祭品转运：由于今年路线涉及临时施工路段，祭祀重物（特别是封口容器及大型牲牢）搬运难度较大，需由专门安保组协助护送，请相关青壮年劳力提前到岗。Offerings Transport: Due to the temporary construction route, moving heavy ritual objects (especially sealed urns and large livestock) is difficult. Dedicated security must assist; relevant personnel must arrive early.
