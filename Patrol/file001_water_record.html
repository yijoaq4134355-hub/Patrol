【节录正文】 [Excerpt Text]

第三章 村落排水系统 Chapter 3: Village Drainage Systems

第二节 鹤田村排水渠 Section 2: Hetian Village Drainage Ditch

一、基本概况1. General Overview

北坡排水渠位于鹤田村北侧山坡下缘，呈东西走向。渠道全长约128米，上口宽约1.2米，底宽约0.8米，深约1.5米，为浆砌石结构，主要用于雨季泄洪及农业引水。 The North Slope Drainage Ditch is located at the lower edge of the north hillside of Hetian Village, running east-west. The channel is about 128 meters long, 1.2 meters wide at the top, 0.8 meters wide at the bottom, and 1.5 meters deep. It is a masonry structure mainly used for flood discharge during the rainy season and agricultural irrigation.

【关键记录】[Key Record]

> 北坡排水渠于1979年修缮完成。The North Slope Drainage Ditch was repaired and completed in 1979.

关键词说明：Keyword Note: 此处使用"修缮"而非"建造"，表明该渠道在1979年前已存在。根据县水利局1973年工程台账，该位置曾有"北坡引水沟"记录，但1973年测绘地图（详见附图）未标注此设施。 The use of "repaired" rather than "built" indicates that the channel existed before 1979. According to the 1973 engineering ledger of the County Water Bureau, there was a record of the "North Slope Diversion Trench" at this location, but the 1973 surveying map (see attached map) did not mark this facility.

三、停用与填埋3. Decommissioning and Landfill

1980年冬，因鹤田村道路建设工程（村道拓宽及硬化），北坡排水渠中段约45米被填埋，改为石填路基。1981年起，该渠道停止维护。 In the winter of 1980, due to the Hetian Village road construction project (widening and hardening of village roads), the middle section of the North Slope Drainage Ditch (about 45 meters) was filled and converted into a stone-filled roadbed. Since 1981, maintenance of the channel has been suspended.
